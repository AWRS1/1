About Us

We are Abdulkareem Almohammad and Sultan AlGhamdi—hydrology experts and robotics engineers. Driven by a passion for safeguarding groundwater, we created AWRS: the first fully autonomous well-inspection robot delivering real-time, high-precision monitoring for water wells worldwide.

The Challenge

  • 🔍 Fewer than 50% of wells operate reliably—corrosion and blockages prevail.
  • ⚠️ 25% of wells are contaminated by fluid or gas leaks, or rusty casings.
  • 🪣 Traditional "bucket & rope" sampling only checks one point and puts crews at risk.

The AWRS Solution

  • 🤖 Autonomous crawler locks onto casings 7″–15″ in diameter.
  • 🔬 XRF + laser pinpoint corrosion and leaks as small as 0.2 mm.
  • 💧 Dual 500 mL & 1 L samplers collect uncontaminated water at surface and depth.
  • 📡 Live video & data streamed via satellite to mobile & web dashboards.

How AWRS Operates

🔧

Deploy & Secure

Hydraulic wheels expand for a rock-solid grip inside the casing.

📐

Scan & Analyze

XRF, laser, and 360° camera capture every inch of the well interior.

💧

Sample Collection

Automated arms extend, grab water, then reseal to prevent cross-contamination.

📲

Stream & Alert

Instant notifications and full data reports at the first sign of trouble.
